Frequently Asked Questions

How does price comparison work?

When you browse a product page on a supported shopping site (Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, Target, Costco, and more), Dart Browser automatically detects the product and adds a Compare option to the menu. Tap it to see the same item across multiple stores — all without leaving the app.

Does Dart Browser collect my browsing data?

No. Dart Browser does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All browsing activity stays entirely on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How does ad blocking work?

Ad blocking is built in and enabled automatically. No configuration is needed. It blocks ads and trackers at the network level, keeping pages fast and clean.

Something isn't working. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, email us at instbiz.app@gmail.com with a brief description of what happened and your device model. We'll look into it promptly.
