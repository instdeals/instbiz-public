Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how InstBrowser (the "App") handles information. We believe that your privacy is a fundamental right, which is why we have designed the App to operate without collecting any of your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Identity Data: Names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • Browsing Data: History, search queries, or cookies.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, device identifiers, or hardware specifications.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates or network-based location information.

2. Data Processing

Because we do not collect any data, we do not process, sell, or rent your information to third parties. All application logic and browsing activity stay strictly local to your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. Any websites you visit while using the App may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices; we recommend reviewing those policies individually.

4. Permissions

The App may request standard browser permissions (such as access to your storage or camera) solely to enable specific functional features you trigger. These permissions are used exclusively to perform the requested task on your device; no data from these actions is ever sent to our servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to check this page periodically for updates.

Questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's privacy practices? Contact us at instbiz.app@gmail.com.
